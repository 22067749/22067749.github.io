The Online World

The provides is introduction to the modern online world and covers topics such as online services, technology and software that support them, digital devices, security and privacy concerns. The internet and web of tomorrow will be even more powerful, more connected, more intuitive and a more important part of our lives. This will result in an internet of services, objects and infrastructure (ubiquitous computing) which will radically change our lives.

Learning aims for this unit are:

A: Investigate online services and online communication.

B: Investigate components of the internet and how digital devices exchange and store information.

C: Investigate issues with operating online.

Technology Systems

For this unit the common hardware components of technology systems such as touch screens and printers, and the internal building blocks of a computer like the processor, buses and memory. The unit also covers the purpose of networks that allow different devices within a technology system to communicate.

Learning aims for this unit are:

A: To understand how the components of technology systems work together.

B: To understand how data flows between internal components of a computer and is processed to provide information.

C: To understand different types of software that are being used in the technology system.
