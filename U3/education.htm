What is Student Voice & What do we do in Student Voice?

Student Voice is a fantastic way for you to get involved in presenting any ideas you or your fellow classmates have to help improve Coleg Cambria.

At Coleg Cambria, I am a student representative. I work with my fellow students, gather feedback on their experiences on their course, and share that feedback with staff, working with them to make the experience better for current and future students.
 I enjoy working on the College's Open Days, where I show people to the relevant departments.
It is a role that comes with much responsibility that I enjoy doing a lot.
My experience as a student rep has encouraged me to put myself forward to be a Student Vice President.
