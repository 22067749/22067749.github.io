Multimedia Products Development

For this unit it explains that in this unit, I had to learn how multimedia products are used and the typical features they contain. I also had to be able to apply some of my findings when creating my own multimedia products. I had to design, develop and test my own multimedia products against a brief that I was given to do this unit.

Learning aims for this unit are:

A: To understanf the uses and features of multimedia products

For this purpose of multimedia products is I had to present content to and engage an audience using a collection of assets, such as video and graphics, to provide an enhanced, multi-sensory, experience  and content can include the different assets: video clips, sound effects, music, animation sequences and images, as well as text.

B: To design multimedia products

I had to design a linear products and non linear products

C: To develop and test multimedia products

I had to use to update the sources table with details of any ready-made assets.
 combine assets to create multimedia products
export and compress assets into suitable file types and sizes

For the testing multimedia products I had to obtain feedback from others, for example, effectiveness, content, presentation, interaction, usability, performance and purpose and make improvements and/or refinements to multimedia products in response to testing and feedback from others.

D: Review the finished multimedia products

Review the finished multimedia products against:
Fitness for purpose
Audience (for a linear product)
User requirements (for an interactive product)
