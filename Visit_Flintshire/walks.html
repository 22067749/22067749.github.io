Walks

You are spoilt for choice in Flintshire with walks for all ages and abilities from challenging climbs to gentle strolls. Offa's Dyke long distance path, which runs the length of Wales from Chepstow in the South to Preststyn on the North coast runns through Flintshire along the Clwydian Range on it's way to the sea.
