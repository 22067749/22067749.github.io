World Wide Web

The World Wide Web is refers to all the public websites or pages that users can access on their local computers and other devices through the internet.

The pages are the primary component or building blocks of the World Wide Web (WWW) and they are linked through hyperlinks which provide access from one specific spot in a hypertext or hypermedia document.

The collection of the web pages belonging to a URL is called a webiste. Like www.techtarget.com is a website.
