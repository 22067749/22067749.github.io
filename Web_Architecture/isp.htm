Internet service provider

The ISP stands for Internet service provider and it's an organization that provides services for accessing, using and managing or the participating in the internet.

ISP works by having an connecton to one or more high speed internet lines. The larger ISPs have their own high speed leased line so they are less dependent on telecommunications services and provide better service to their customers.

ISPs provide the following internet service: Cable Fiber DSL Satellite that do give the customers their internet from the ISPs provides
